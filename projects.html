Northwestern University

As my senior thesis, I created a method to model the power variations from spinning a half-wave plate for the TolTEC polarimetry camera. The TolTEC is currently being manufactured for the LMT as a collaboration based at UMass Amherst. The source code for the synchronous signal analysis can be found on my GitHub.

I also worked with polarimetry data from the SOFIA airborne observatory. I created a method to compare the new SOFIA data against known Herschel data to check for correct pointing and astrometry
